About Me

Luna History

My name is Luna and I am a German Sheperd mix. I was born in the Bakersfield/Fresno area but then my sister and I were brought up to the Bay Area and stayed at the Family Dog Rescue Shelter. We were there a couple of weeks before we were let out to meet people.

Within the first week of being able to see people, a nice family of three young people came in and picked myself and my sister as pets they thought they might want. We were still tired from our traveling so we did not do much, but the family liked how mellow we were. They wanted to take my sister and I together but the shelter would not let them take both of us.

So, off I went, with my new family. Now I am almost one year old and I am having a great time. I love being outside, running around, playing fetch, biting whatever I can get my teeth on and getting my belly rubbed.
